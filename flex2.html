IBACCO

Chocolate ice cream sundae

R.G. Chandramogan is the owner of Ibaco ice cream, having founded it as part of his larger Hatsun.
He started with an ice candy business and built it into a dairy empire, expanding to launch
Ibaco parlors alongside his other brands like Arun Ice Creams and Arokya.


Ibaco is an Indian ice cream brand from Hatsun Agro Product Ltd. that offers a wide variety of flavors and
personalized options, including sundaes, ice cream cakes, and bars. It is known for 36 unique, world-inspired flavors, with options
to customize with various toppings and sauces. The brand is dedicated to providing a high-quality and diverse ice cream experience.
